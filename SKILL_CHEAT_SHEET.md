## ğŸ’¡ General Patterns

### To Use Any Skill Explicitly:
> "Use [skill-name] to [task]"

Example: *"Use prisma to design the user schema"*

### To Let Me Choose:
> "What's the best skill for [problem]?"

Example: *"What's the best skill for handling database migrations?"*

### To Check What's Available:
> "Check the skills registry" or "What skills do we have for [topic]?"

### For Complex Work:
> "Spawn a sub-agent with [skill] to [task]"

Example: *"Spawn a sub-agent with docker-essentials to optimize the Dockerfile"*
